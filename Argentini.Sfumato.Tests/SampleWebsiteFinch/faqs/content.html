5) WHY THE NAME ‘FINCH’?

Finches are one of the most adaptable, resilient creatures on the planet. Gregarious and bright, their simple song unites flocks of hundreds, called charms (how charming!). They are a symbol of joy, energy, and brighter times ahead. They love the wild, but also flourish in urban areas, and of course, eat local. This prolific little bird famously studied by Darwin has shown us how we can make small changes to adapt and thrive in an unpredictable world. We also can’t say ‘finch’ without thinking of Atticus Finch, a fictional character synonymous with integrity, accountability, and a better life for all.

6) DO YOU TAKE COMMISSION FOR REVIEWS?

Nope! To maintain our unbiased rating system, we do not accept payment from companies for reviews or product placement.

7) WHAT’S THE CHARM?

The Charm is Finch’s ambassador program, through which vetted individuals and families are deemed ‘Finch Finders’ that provide candid feedback about how well products work, how long they last, and how enjoyable they are to use. Finders’ real reviews are a component of the “Using It” score within all Finch ratings. There are thousands of products out there, and our Charm of Finders help to test everything for functionality and quality – combining their real reviews with the real science is how we find the best of the best.

8) HOW DOES FINCH MAKE MONEY?

Our first priority is to maintain the integrity of our data and insights. Companies neither pay us to be included in our tools, nor to get better scores. We always do the digging and testing first, but sometimes brands like what they see and offer us an affiliate fee to link out to their page in our Wise Guides. We’re still a young company and, when we scale, our main source of revenue will come from working with companies to help them focus on what matters and provide anonymized, aggregate intel on what we’ve learned from the Charm. We will never share individuals’ information, purchasing history, or browsing habits.
