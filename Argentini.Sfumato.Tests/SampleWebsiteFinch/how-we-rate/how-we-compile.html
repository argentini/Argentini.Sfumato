How we compile our insights

Combining function and scientific fact is how we find the best of the best

Finch is powered by a proprietary algorithm that puts products through the wringer by assessing what they’re made of, how they’re created, how long they last, and everything in between.

Our custom technology cuts through the greenwash of today’s consumer products, analyzing company-level and product-level data and accounting for a variety of interconnected environmental and social factors across five key areas. Instead of just saying one product is more “sustainable” than another, we simplify the science, category by category. No jargon, no blanket claims or wishy-washy statements.
